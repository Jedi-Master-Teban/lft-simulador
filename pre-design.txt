SECTION 1 — PRODUCT OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Name          : Simulador de Jornada Laboral
Owned by      : Catch Consulting, S.C. — HR Consultancy & Organizational Development
Website       : www.catchconsulting.com.mx
Context       : Delivered as part of Catch's "Taller 40 Horas" training workshop

What it does  :
  A B2B compliance tool for Mexican manufacturing companies. HR managers enter
  their factory crew schedules (shift start/end times for each day of the week)
  and the tool tells them whether they comply with Mexico's Federal Labor Law (LFT)
  — specifically the constitutional reform reducing the maximum workweek from 48h
  to 40h between 2026 and 2030.

  The tool calculates regular hours, "horas dobles" (2× overtime), and
  "horas triples" (3× overtime), checks four compliance rules per crew, and
  estimates the weekly overtime cost per crew and for the whole company.

Who uses it   :
  HR managers and labor compliance specialists at tier-1 automotive and
  industrial manufacturing companies in Mexico.
  Representative clients: BMW, Honda, Toyota, Bosch, Continental, Valeo,
  Metalsa, Nemak, Lear, ZF, Magna, BorgWarner, Johnson Controls.

Stakes        :
  Non-compliance means real labor violations and fines. The tool is used to
  plan workforce scheduling changes before the legal deadlines hit.

Tech stack    :
  React 19 + Vite + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS v4
  lucide-react (icons), jsPDF + jspdf-autotable (PDF export), SheetJS (Excel export)
  Single-page app, no router, data persisted in localStorage.
